About Me

I'm a Student

I am a student majoring in English Education at Bengkulu University. I am a prospective teacher who is passionate and committed to making a positive contribution to the world of education. Since starting this journey, I've realized that teaching is not just about conveying information, but also about building supportive relationships with students, giving them space to grow, and inspiring them to reach their full potential.
Since starting my studies, I have felt truly inspired by a teacher's ability to guide and motivate students. Therefore, I am committed to continuing to learn and hone my pedagogical skills through various practicum experiences, training, and direct interactions with students. I believe that a good education is not just about transferring knowledge, but also about creating deep relationships with students, understanding their needs, and helping them develop to their best potential.
As I delve deeper into this field, my focus is on honing my skills, not only for academic success, but also to make a real impact in the world. My passion for learning and drive to innovate inspires me to make meaningful contributions to the education industry. Whether through developing learning methods, helping create a fun learning environment, or being a good role model for my future students, I am determined to achieve success in this area and continue to grow.
